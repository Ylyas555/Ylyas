My Country Turkmenistan

Turkmenistan is my home country, a country rich in history and culture, is located in Central Asia. Known for its stunning landscapes, from the vast Karakum Desert to the beautiful Caspian Sea coast, our country boasts a unique blend of tradition and modernity. The warmth of the Turkmen people is reflected in our hospitality, which is a cornerstone of our culture. Our customs are deeply rooted in ancient traditions, with a strong emphasis on family and community. The art of carpet weaving, recognized globally, showcases our craftsmanship and storytelling, as each carpet tells a tale of our heritage.
