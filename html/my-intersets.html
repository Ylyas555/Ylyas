Exploring My Interests

  • Comparative Religion

My fascination with comparative religion stems from a deep curiosity about the beliefs and practices that shape cultures around the globe. Studying various religions allows me to explore the fundamental questions of existence, morality, and purpose. I am particularly interested in how different faiths address similar themes, such as compassion, justice, and the human experience. Although talking about religion and politics can put you in a trouble, I disagree with Gabriel Iglesias in his quote.

  • Politics

Politics is another area that captivates my attention. The dynamics of power, governance, and social change are crucial in shaping our societies. I am particularly interested in the intersection of politics and religion, examining how belief systems influence political ideologies and policies. This intersection is especially relevant in today's world, where discussions about rights, freedoms, and justice are at the forefront of public discourse.

  • Sports

Sports have played a significant role in my life, shaping my values and interests. My favorite sport is soccer, a game that brings together not only athleticism but also teamwork and strategy. I enjoy both playing and watching it, as each match is a blend of skill, passion, and excitement.
